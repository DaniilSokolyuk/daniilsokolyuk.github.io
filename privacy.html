Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all applications developed and published by Daniil Sokolyuk. This policy describes what information is collected, how it is used, and what choices you have.

What information do we collect?

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Our applications do not require registration, login, or any form of personal data entry. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal details.

How do we use your information?

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use it in any way. Any data processed by our applications (such as device sensor readings or user preferences) remains entirely on your device.

How do we protect your information?

We do not use vulnerability scanning and/or scanning to PCI standards. We never ask for credit card numbers. We do not use Malware Scanning. All communication between our applications and external services uses standard encrypted protocols.

Do we use cookies?

Our applications do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies.

Third-party disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. Our applications do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Third-party links

Occasionally, at our discretion, we may include links to third-party products or services within our applications. These third-party sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites.

Device permissions

Some of our applications may request access to device features such as Bluetooth, camera, or local file storage. These permissions are used solely for core application functionality. All data processed through these features stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

CalOPPA (California Online Privacy Protection Act)

According to CalOPPA, we agree to the following:

  • Users can visit our applications anonymously
  • This privacy policy link is accessible from our application store listings
  • Users will be notified of any privacy policy changes on this page

COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

We do not specifically market to children under the age of 13. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Fair Information Practices

In order to be in line with Fair Information Practices, should a data breach occur we will notify users via in-app notification within 7 business days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact Daniil Sokolyuk at d@sokoly.uk.
